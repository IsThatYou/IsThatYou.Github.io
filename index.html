About Me

I am a fourth-year Computer Science and Mathemtics undergrad at UC Irvine, where I work closely with Sameer Singh on Machine Learning Interpretaions and Natural Language Processing Projects.

Previously I summer interned in Applied AI lab at Comcast in DC, and I was supervised by Ferhan Ture.

My research experience has been prmiarily on adversarial methods and interpretations of NLP models. But I am very interested in all kind of NLP projects, including dialog systems or language models.

I am also part of the ACM@UCI as I enjoy solving programming problems!
